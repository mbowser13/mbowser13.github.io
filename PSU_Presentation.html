Back in the summer of 2012 I worked as a GIS Technician for the Carnegie Museum of Natural History. While working for the museum I was tasked with creating a series of trail maps for the Powdermill Nature Reserve, which is owned by the museum. One of these maps was for the Black Birch Trail, which is directly adjacent to the Powdermill Nature Center.
